Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum data required to provide you with timely government job notifications. Below is a breakdown of what we may collect:

📱
Device Token Your FCM push notification token, used solely to deliver alerts to your device.
📍
State Preference The state(s) you select to filter relevant job notifications for your region.
🔖
Bookmarks Jobs you save are stored locally on your device. We don't store them on our servers.
📊
Usage Analytics Anonymous app usage data (screens visited, session duration) via Firebase Analytics.
⚙️
Device Info OS version, device model, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
🔍
Search Queries Anonymous search terms to improve job discovery features within the app.

We do NOT collect: your name, email address, phone number, Aadhaar, or any other personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it when contacting support.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected for the following legitimate purposes only:

  • Send push notifications about new government job postings, exam results, and admit cards.
  • Personalize job alerts based on your selected state, category, or education preferences.
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience through anonymous analytics.
  • Understand which job categories are most popular to prioritize content updates.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the app and our backend services.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising or selling to advertisers.
  • We do not build personal profiles or track your behaviour outside our app.
  • We do not share your data with recruitment agencies or employers.

Section 03

Push Notifications

Push notifications are the core feature of Sarkari Job Alert. When you install the app and grant permission, we use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you real-time alerts about:

  • New government job postings from central and state boards.
  • Application deadlines and last date reminders.
  • Exam admit card release and result declarations.
  • Answer key publications and syllabus updates.

🔕 You can opt out of notifications at any time through your device settings (Settings → Apps → Sarkari Job Alert → Notifications) or within the app under More → Notifications.

Section 05

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect it.

  • All data transmission between the app and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Push notification tokens are stored securely and are not accessible to unauthorized parties.
  • Bookmarks and local preferences are stored on your device only, not on our servers.
  • We conduct periodic security reviews of our backend infrastructure.
  • Firebase services are hosted on Google Cloud, which complies with ISO 27001 and SOC 2 standards.

Data Retention: We retain anonymous usage analytics for up to 14 months as permitted by Firebase. Push notification tokens are kept active as long as the app is installed and deleted upon uninstall. We do not retain personally identifiable data beyond what is necessary.

Section 06

Your Rights & Choices

As a user of Sarkari Job Alert, you have the following rights and controls over your data:

  • Disable notifications — Turn off alerts at any time via device settings or inside the app.
  • Clear local data — Delete all locally stored preferences by clearing app data from your device settings.
  • Uninstall — Removing the app stops all data collection and deletes your notification token from our servers within 30 days.
  • Request data deletion — Contact us at the email below to request deletion of any data we may hold related to your device.
  • Opt out of analytics — You can limit ad tracking and analytics through your Android device's privacy settings.

Indian Users: We comply with applicable Indian data protection principles. If you have questions about how your data is handled under Indian law, please contact us directly.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

Sarkari Job Alert is intended for users who are at least 18 years of age, as government job eligibility in India generally requires applicants to be adults.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.
